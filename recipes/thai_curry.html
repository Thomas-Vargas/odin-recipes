Thai Red Curry

Description

As you can see in the image above, most Thai curries start out with a similar flavor base consisting of lemongrass, galangal, shrimp paste, and other aromatics pounded into a smooth paste. Layered on top of those ingredients are the flavors that make each curry unique.This red curry is bright and heavily spiced with dried red chilis.

Ingredients

  • Thai rice
  • Lemongrass
  • Thai basil
  • Red curry paste
  • Red bell pepper
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Coconut milk
  • Kaffir lime
  • Chicken breast/thighs

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in medium pot. Add rice and 1 tbsp of butter. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Add curry paste to large pan and heat for 2-3 minutes. Add coconut milk and stir till combined. Add chicken chunks and cook for 8 minutes.
  3. Chop bell pepper and add to pan, along with bamboo shoots, kaffir lime and lemongrass. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serbe curry on top of rice. Enjoy!
