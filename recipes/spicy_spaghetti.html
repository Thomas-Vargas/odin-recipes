That's a Spicy Spaghetti!

Description

Classic spaghetti and meat sauce but with a little heat! Optional fresh mozzarella to balance out the heat and add some flavor. Delecioso!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Red pepper flake
  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil. Add pasta and 1 tbsp of olive oil to keep seperated. Boil for 10 minutes or unti al dente.
  2. Add ground beef to large pan at medium heat. Cook until brown.
  3. Remove beef from pan and drain excess oil.
  4. Add 3 tbsp of olive oil to same pan and add 4 tbsp of red pepper flakes. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add spaghetti sauce and ground beef to pan. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve sauce on top of spaghetti. Add basil and mozzarella as desired.
