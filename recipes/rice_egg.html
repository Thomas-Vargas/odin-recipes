Delicious Rice Egg!

Description

This is a simple recipe made under dire conditions during the pandemic. Very simple ingredients and easy prep with lots of flavor!

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Dried Basil
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil, adding 1-2tbsp of butter and a tsp of dried basil. Add rice and simmer for 20 minutes or until water is gone.
  2. Scramble 2-3 eggs on medium heat, breaking the yolk halfway through to keep moist.
  3. Fluff rice with fork and serve in bowl with eggs on top
  4. Add salt/pepper to taste and add desired amount of soy sauce.
